# 🤝 Contributing

Thank you for your interest in SOMA! 🎉

---

## 📋 Current Contribution Policy

SOMA is a personal/academic project under active development. At this stage, **pull requests are not being accepted**. This is not a reflection of your work — it's a risk management decision to maintain control over a system that handles sensitive student data.

If a PR is opened, it will be reviewed and most likely closed. This policy may change in future versions.

---

## 💬 Feedback and Suggestions

Feedback is genuinely welcome. If you have ideas, spotted something odd, or want to suggest improvements:

- Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/QuantumRevenant/SOMA/discussions) if available
- Or reach out directly: [@QuantumRevenant on X](https://twitter.com/QuantumRevenant)

---

## 🐛 Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or unexpected behavior:

1. Check [existing issues](https://github.com/QuantumRevenant/SOMA/issues) to avoid duplicates
2. Open a new issue with:
   - What happened
   - Steps to reproduce
   - What you expected instead
   - Any relevant logs, screenshots, or error messages
   - Your environment (OS, browser, Docker version)
